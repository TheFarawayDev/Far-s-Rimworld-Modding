[h1]Quarry Co.[/h1]
[i]Dig in. Literally.[/i]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Overview[/h2]
Quarry Co. adds seven sizes of quarry pits that let your colonists extract stone chunks and minerals from the ground. No more strip-mining the entire map for granite. Just build a quarry and assign miners. Spawned resources are automatically marked for hauling.

[h2]Buildings[/h2]

[b]Small Quarry (3x3)[/b]
[list]
[*] Cost: 30 wood, 10 steel
[*] Up to 9 miners at once
[*] Yields stone chunks, steel, slag, silver, and components
[/list]

[b]Quarry (5x5)[/b]
[list]
[*] Cost: 80 wood, 30 steel
[*] Up to 25 miners at once
[*] Same drops as small, plus gold, jade, plasteel, and uranium
[/list]

[b]Large Quarry (7x7)[/b]
[list]
[*] Cost: 150 wood, 60 steel, 2 components
[*] Up to 49 miners at once
[*] Great drop rates for rare materials
[/list]

[b]Grand Quarry (9x9)[/b]
[list]
[*] Cost: 250 wood, 120 steel, 4 components
[*] Up to 81 miners at once
[*] Requires Advanced Quarrying research (Industrial tier, 1200 cost)
[*] High drop rates for plasteel, uranium, gold, jade, and components
[*] Construction skill 6 required
[/list]

[b]Colossal Quarry (11x11)[/b]
[list]
[*] Cost: 400 wood, 250 steel, 8 components
[*] Up to 121 miners at once
[*] Insanely low stone drops, very high ore and steel drops
[*] Construction skill 8 required
[/list]

[b]Titan Quarry (13x13)[/b]
[list]
[*] Cost: 600 wood, 400 steel, 15 components
[*] Up to 169 miners at once
[*] Almost no stone waste, massively increased ore yields
[*] Construction skill 10 required
[/list]

[b]Omega Quarry (15x15)[/b]
[list]
[*] Cost: 900 wood, 600 steel, 25 components
[*] Up to 225 miners at once
[*] Pure ore extraction with zero stone chunks, primarily steel and precious metals
[*] Construction skill 12 required
[/list]

[h2]How It Works[/h2]
[list]
[*] Build a quarry (requires Quarrying research, 400 cost, Neolithic tier)
[*] Pawns assigned to Mining will automatically work the quarry
[*] Multiple pawns can mine the same quarry simultaneously (1 per tile)
[*] Each mining cycle produces a random resource based on quarry size
[*] Resources spawn unforbidden with a haul designation
[*] Toggle quarrying on/off with the gizmo button
[/list]

[h2]Settings[/h2]
Configurable in mod settings:
[list]
[*] Workers per tile multiplier (default 1.0x, range 0.1-3.0)
[*] Auto-haul quarried resources (default on)
[*] Minimum mining skill (default 0, range 0-20)
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Vanilla-friendly[/b] - no DLC required
[*] [b]Safe to add mid-save[/b]
[*] [b]RimWorld 1.6[/b]
[/list]
